The Best Jerseys of Each Year (2001-2016)

Barcelona 2009 Home Kit

Possibly the most iconic kit of all time. This kit is great, simple, and memorable. not much else to be said.

World Cup 2010 Spain Home kit

A great kit fit for the world cup winners. Spanish kits always look very solid, but this is by far one of their best.

Liverpool 2011 home kit

Liverpool has some iconic kits, but this kit is great to me. just feels like simple liverpool and a great overall kit.

Chelsea 2012 Home kit

Maybe one of my favorite prem kits of all time, this Chelsea kit is riddled with nostalgia and memories. Defines that era of Chelsea extremely well, especially in association with Didier Drogba

2013 bayern munich kit

Peak Bayern. a great, incredible simple kit that was worn through the club's best times is near unrivaled in the clubs history.

Argentina 2014 away kit

While not the winners of this world cup, the finalists did have an incredible jersey. the dark blue is absolutely incredible on, it resulting in a jersey that may not be as iconic as the home kit, but is certainly as well made.

Juventus 2015 kit

Juventus have always had good jerseys, but the black and gold on the 2015 kit truly stood out to me as something for the ages.

2016 Real Madrid home kit

An incredible kit for real madrid's champions league winning year, a classic and sleek design makes this an unforgettable kit for madrid.
