The Best Jerseys of Each Year (2001-2016)

PSG 2017 Kit

The Best kit I've seen from PSG, iconic and memorable during an era where they had many incrdible players

2018 Real Madrid away

The Kit worn when Madrid completed three back to back to back UCLs, an iconic kit from the spanish giants.

Ajax Away 2019 Kit

Ajax's insane champion's league campaign was unforgettable. While they did lose against Spurs in the end, the talent on that team was undeniable, and this kit was just as good

Inter 2020 home kit

A good pattern, color scheme, and design makes this jersey a standout in an odd era of the sport.

2021 Belgium Euro Kit

A great looking kit for the belgians, one of my personal favorite euro kits ever.

Japan 2022 home kit

While not the most iconic kit of the recent world cup, this Japan Kit is nothing short of stunning, and a memorable piece of their world cup campaign.

Juventus 2015 kit

A club from outside the Top 5 leagues has made it in with a great checkered oattern and good color scheme. PSV made a memorable kit that looks incredible.

2024 Real Madrid kit

A great season so far for madrid, and this kit is great as well. a nice black and dark grey design is a consistent theme for Madrid's second and third kits, but is especially done well on this kit.
