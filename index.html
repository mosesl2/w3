The Best Jerseys of Each Year (2001-2016)

Man United 2000-2001 Home Jersey

Coming off a great season, the kits of 2001 did not dissapoint, making an iconic home kit that has memories associated with it

World Cup 2002 Brazil First Jersey

One of the most iconic kits of all time, the World Cup winning Brazil jersey is near perfect.

Arsenal 2003-2004 Home kit

The kit of the season Arsenal went invincible. possibly the most iconic premier league kit of all time.

Euro 2004 Portugal kit

The 2004 Euro finalists had a great kit this year. most famously worn by Ronaldo and Figo, this kit is one of the best kits the euro has produced.

2005 Ac Milan Home Kit

While they did lose the champions league going up 3-0, this kit is still incredible. giving off everything a Milan kit should.

France 2006 World Cup away kit

A kit most associated with Zidane and his final performance, this kit was an incredible product of the 2006 world cup.

Ac Milan 2007 kit

The first team to be on here twice, but this kit is too iconic to not feature. another memorable kit from Milan.

2008 Man United home kit

Just an iconic kit. One of the kits most associated with Ronaldo, this kit was absolutely stunning and is still one of my favorites.
